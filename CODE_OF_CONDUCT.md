# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of technical knowledge.

## Expected behaviour

Community members are expected to:

- communicate with empathy, patience, and respect;
- give and receive technical feedback constructively;
- focus disagreement on ideas and evidence, not people;
- respect privacy and confidentiality; and
- help maintain a safe environment for contributors and users.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualised attention,
personal attacks, deliberate intimidation, publishing another person's private information, and
continued disruption after a maintainer asks that it stop.

## Scope

This code applies in project repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, discussions, and other
spaces where someone is representing the project or Elasyn.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact channel at
[elasyn.com.au](https://elasyn.com.au). Do not include personal information beyond what is needed
to investigate the report, and do not open a public issue about a sensitive incident.

Maintainers will review reports in good faith, protect the privacy and safety of those involved as
far as practical, and take action proportionate to the circumstances. Actions may include a private
warning, removal of content, temporary restrictions, or a permanent ban from project spaces.

Retaliation against someone who reports a concern in good faith is not acceptable.
